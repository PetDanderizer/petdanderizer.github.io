Hamster facts

Hamsters are:
  • Cute
  • Fuzzy
  • Only mildly murderous
When meeting a hamster, do the following:
  1. Pet it
  2. Hold it close
  3. Try your best to keep it away from nearby live power cords - hamsters find these delicious
